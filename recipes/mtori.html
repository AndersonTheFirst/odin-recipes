Mtori

Description

During my time in Tanzania, I was exposed to many dishes. However, one of my favorite was Mtori. This is delicious dish is hails from the WaChagga people at the foot of Mt. Kilimanjaro Although it's a stew, don't let its looks decieve you. Mtori is bursting with flavor and very filling.

Ingredients

  • Beep pieces with bones (1 kg)
  • Plantain Bananas
  • Large potato (1)
  • Large Onion (1)
  • Blueband (3 tbs)
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Put beef pieces in a pot of water (enough water to cover all the meat) and boil until cooked
  2. Then remove the meat from heat, remove the meat from soup
  3. Peel bananas and chop, cut into pieces and wash
  4. Put a banana in a water pan and salt, Sprinkle onions , carrot and bell pepper and add them to a banana pan
  5. Boil until bananas are tender, Regularly check and stir the plantains to make sure they are tender enough
  6. Remove the boiled plantains from heat, then Crush/blend the banana with hand while adding the meat soup, to soften it
  7. Add blueband/tanbond while crushing the banana, Add more soup until the desired consistency, Add the meat in mtori.
  8. Serve mtori with lime and pepper
